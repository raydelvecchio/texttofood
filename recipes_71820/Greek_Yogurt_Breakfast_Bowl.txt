Recipe: Greek Yogurt Breakfast Bowl
Description: A nutritious and satisfying breakfast bowl topped with fresh fruit and a drizzle of honey.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 count banana
- 0.5 cup blueberries
- 1 tbsp honey
- 0.25 cup granola

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, add Greek yogurt as the base.
2. Slice the banana and layer it on top of the yogurt.
3. Add blueberries on top of the banana slices.
4. Drizzle honey over the entire bowl.
5. Sprinkle granola on top for added crunch and enjoyment.
