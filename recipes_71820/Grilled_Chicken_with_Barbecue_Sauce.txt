Recipe: Grilled Chicken with Barbecue Sauce
Description: Grilled chicken pieces coated with a homemade barbecue sauce for a sweet and tangy flavor.

Ingredients:
- 1 None whole chicken
- 2 tbsp butter
- 0.33 cup ketchup
- 0.25 cup lemon juice
- 0.25 cup soy sauce
- 1 tbsp honey
- 0.5 tsp black pepper
- 0.5 tsp hot sauce

Instructions:
1. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat. Stir in ketchup, lemon juice, soy sauce, honey, black pepper, and hot sauce.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for about 15 minutes until reduced.
3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat, and prepare the chicken pieces by spraying the grill rack with nonstick spray.
4. Grill the chicken skin side down, covered, for 20-30 minutes, turning occasionally and basting with leftover sauce until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
