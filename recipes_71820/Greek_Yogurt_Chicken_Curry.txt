Recipe: Greek Yogurt Chicken Curry
Description: A creamy and flavorful chicken curry dish that uses Greek yogurt for a healthier twist.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1 count onion
- 3 clove garlic
- 1 inch ginger
- 2 tbsp curry powder
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 0.5 salt
- 0.25 black pepper

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
2. Add diced onion, minced garlic, and ginger; saut√© until softened.
3. Add chicken, curry powder, salt, and pepper; cook until chicken is browned.
4. Pour in coconut milk and let simmer for 10-15 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
5. Remove from heat and stir in Greek yogurt until well mixed.
6. Serve hot with rice or naan.
