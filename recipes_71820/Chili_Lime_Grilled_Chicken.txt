Recipe: Chili Lime Grilled Chicken
Description: Moist and juicy chicken thighs marinated in a chili lime mixture for a refreshing flavor.

Ingredients:
- 2 lb chicken thighs
- 0.5 cup lime juice
- 3 tsp lime zest
- 0.25 cup olive oil
- 4 tbsp cilantro
- 2 None jalapeno
- 4 clove garlic
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp chili powder

Instructions:
1. Rinse and pat dry the chicken thighs, set aside.
2. In a bowl, whisk together lime juice, lime zest, olive oil, cilantro, minced jalapenos, minced garlic, honey, salt, and chili powder to create the marinade.
3. Place the chicken in the marinade and let it sit, preferably for 2 hours in the fridge.
4. Preheat the grill and lightly oil the grates before placing the chicken on it.
5. Grill until golden brown and charred on both sides, about 5-7 minutes per side or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
