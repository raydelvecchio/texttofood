Recipe: Moroccan Meatballs with Yogurt Sauce
Description: Juicy meatballs flavored with spices, served with a refreshing Greek yogurt sauce.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 0.5 salt
- 0.25 black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 count cucumber
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp fresh dill

Instructions:
1. Preheat the grill or oven to 400Â°F.
2. In a bowl, mix ground beef with cumin, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Form the mixture into meatballs and place them on a baking sheet or grill.
4. Cook meatballs for about 20 minutes, turning halfway through.
5. Meanwhile, make the yogurt sauce by mixing Greek yogurt with diced cucumber, dill, salt, and pepper.
6. Serve meatballs warm with the yogurt sauce on the side.
