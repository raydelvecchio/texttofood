Recipe: Parmesan Baked Chicken Breast
Description: Juicy and tender chicken breasts marinated with olive oil, garlic powder, and lemon juice, topped with Parmesan cheese and baked to perfection.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1.5 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- ground black pepper
- 3 dashes cayenne pepper
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.5 cup Parmesan cheese
- lemon wedges

Instructions:
1. Marinate the chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic powder, lemon juice, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and salt. The marinade will be paste-y and not liquidy.
2. While the chicken breasts are marinating, preheat oven to 400°F (207°C).
3. Top the marinated chicken breasts with the grated Parmesan cheese evenly.
4. Bake the chicken for about 20-30 minutes, until a meat thermometer hits 165°F when inserted into the thickest part of the breasts.
5. Remove the Parmesan baked chicken from the oven. Serve immediately with fresh lemon wedges.
