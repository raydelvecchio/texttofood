Recipe: Whipped Greek Yogurt with Honey
Description: A quick and easy dessert option thatâ€™s rich and satisfying, made with whipped Greek yogurt.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp honey
- 0.5 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. In a mixing bowl, combine Greek yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract.
2. Using a hand mixer, whip until light and fluffy.
3. Serve in small bowls with an extra drizzle of honey on top if desired.
