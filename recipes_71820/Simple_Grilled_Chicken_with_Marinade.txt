Recipe: Simple Grilled Chicken with Marinade
Description: A quick and easy marinade for tender, juicy grilled chicken breasts.

Ingredients:
- 24 oz boneless chicken breast
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp pepper
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 4 tbsp acid (lime or lemon juice)

Instructions:
1. Combine paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
2. Rinse and pat dry the chicken breasts, then dredge them in the spice mixture.
3. In a separate bowl, mix olive oil and acid of your choice together, then add to the chicken, ensuring it's well coated.
4. Let the chicken sit in the marinade while you prep the grill, for up to 15 minutes.
5. Preheat the grill to high, then reduce to medium-high before grilling the chicken.
6. Place chicken on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side.
7. Check doneness using a meat thermometer, remove when it reaches 160Â°F and let rest in foil for a few minutes before serving.
