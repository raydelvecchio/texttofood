Recipe: Cajun Baked Chicken Breast
Description: Moist chicken breasts coated with a creamy Cajun seasoning and baked with cheese on top.

Ingredients:
- 3 lbs chicken breast
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 3 tbsp Cajun seasoning
- 0.75 cup cheddar cheese
- 0.25 cup parsley
- 0.5 cup bread crumbs

Instructions:
1. Stir together mayonnaise, Cajun seasoning, cheese, and parsley.
2. Spread the mixture evenly on chicken breasts. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top.
3. Place chicken breasts on a sprayed baking sheet. Bake at 425Â°F for 20 minutes or until cooked through.
