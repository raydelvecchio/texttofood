Recipe: Baked Cornish Hen
Description: Tender and juicy Cornish hen baked with a marinade of garlic, honey, and sambal oelek, perfect for a special dinner.

Ingredients:
- 1 whole cornish hen
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 0.5 tbsp sambal oelek
- 1 tsp salt

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Rinse and pat dry the cornish hen.
2. Stuff the cavity with garlic. Combine olive oil, honey, sambal oelek, and salt to create the marinade. Brush it on the hen.
3. Place on a roasting pan and bake for 60 minutes, turning halfway.
