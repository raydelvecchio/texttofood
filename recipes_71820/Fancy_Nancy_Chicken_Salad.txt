Recipe: Fancy Nancy Chicken Salad
Description: A delightful and fruity chicken salad that features grapes, apples, and pecans for a delicious twist. Perfect for a light lunch or a nature outing.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lb chicken tenders
- 4 cup chicken stock
- 0.75 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp dry ranch dressing mix
- 0.5 cup celery
- 0.75 cup red seedless grapes
- 0.5 cup Fuji apple
- 0.5 cup chopped pecans
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp black pepper

Instructions:
1. Poach chicken tenders in chicken stock until fully cooked. Shred once cooled.
2. In a mixing bowl, combine mayonnaise, ranch dressing mix, salt, and pepper.
3. Fold in the shredded chicken, diced celery, grapes, chopped apple, and pecans.
4. Chill for at least one hour before serving on lettuce or in a sandwich.
