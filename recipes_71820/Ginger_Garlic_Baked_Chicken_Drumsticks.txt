Recipe: Ginger Garlic Baked Chicken Drumsticks
Description: Tender and juicy chicken drumsticks marinated with ginger, garlic, soy sauce, and honey, baked to perfection.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lb chicken drumsticks
- 2 inch ginger
- 5 cloves garlic
- 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
- 0.5 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 3 dashes white pepper
- 1 pinch Chinese five-spice powder
- 1 pinch salt

Instructions:
1. Rinse the chicken and pat dry. Rub ginger and garlic on the chicken.
2. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey, sesame oil, white pepper, and Chinese five-spice powder. Mix to coat the chicken well.
3. Marinate for at least 30 minutes or best for 2 hours in the fridge.
4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Arrange chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
5. Bake for 30-40 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
