Recipe: Greek Yogurt Popsicles
Description: A healthy, homemade popsicle thatâ€™s easy to make and perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 cup mixed frozen berries

Instructions:
1. In a blender, combine Greek yogurt, honey, lemon juice, and frozen berries.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Pour the mixture into popsicle molds, leaving a little space at the top.
4. Insert sticks and freeze until solid, about 6 hours.
5. To remove, run warm water over the outside of the mold before pulling out the popsicles.
