Recipe: Traditional Chicken Salad with a Twist
Description: A classic chicken salad updated with green peppers, fennel seeds, and a homemade mayonnaise dressing. This salad is perfect for picnics, potlucks, or a refreshing meal on its own.

Ingredients:
- 1.5 lb chicken breast
- 1 None green peppers
- 1 tsp fennel seeds
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 0.5 cup mayonnaise
- 4 oz mixed greens
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 0.5 tsp black pepper

Instructions:
1. Cook chicken breast and let cool. Chop into small cubes.
2. In a bowl, mix together chopped chicken, diced green peppers, fennel seeds, lemon juice, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
3. Serve on a bed of mixed greens. Optionally, refrigerate before serving.
