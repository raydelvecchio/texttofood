Recipe: Chicken Cordon Bleu Casserole
Description: An easy casserole of chicken breasts layered with ham and cheese, baked in cream of mushroom soup for a quick weeknight meal.

Ingredients:
- 6 pieces skinless boneless chicken breasts
- 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
- 0.5 cup milk
- 6 pieces deli ham slices
- 6 slices Monterey Jack cheese
- salt
- black pepper

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Combine soup and milk in a baking dish. Lay chicken breasts in the dish, seasoning with salt and pepper.
2. Layer slices of ham and cheese over the chicken. Bake for 40-45 minutes.
