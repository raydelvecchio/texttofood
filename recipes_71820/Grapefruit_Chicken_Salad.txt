Recipe: Grapefruit Chicken Salad
Description: A refreshing salad combining chicken with grapefruit, chili pepper, and a homemade dressing. It's a unique and zesty meal option packed with flavor.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb chicken breast
- 1 None grapefruit
- 400 g mixed salad greens
- 1 None chili pepper
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 4 tbsp mustard sauce
- 4 clove garlic
- 1 None lemon
- 8 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp salt

Instructions:
1. Boil the chicken breast until cooked, then cool and slice.
2. Prepare salad greens and place in a large bowl.
3. Remove the peel from the grapefruit and chop into chunks.
4. Chop the chili pepper, add all ingredients but do not mix yet.
5. Make dressing with minced garlic, honey, mustard, soy sauce, olive oil, and lemon juice; combine well.
6. Pour dressing over salad ingredients and mix before serving.
